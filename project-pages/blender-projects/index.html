3D MODELING PROJECTS

An Empathy Test

I'm by no means a serious 3D artist. But I think that, as a technologist whose goal is to help create procedural tools that will hopefully be used by artists, it's important to understand some of the problems firsthand. So I work on projects every now and then to better understand computer graphics from an artist's perspective. Below are some of those projects.

SG (Original)

A guitar that I modeled in Blender. People have pointed out that I put the "Gibson" label on the guitar, but it has an Epiphone headstock. I didn't realize the headstocks were different!

Anvil (Tutorial)

I followed Andrew "BlenderGuru" Price's anvil tutorial to model this in Blender.
